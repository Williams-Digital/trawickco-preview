About Us

A Legacy of Quality
Construction

Trawick Contractors Inc. was established in 1954 as "Dan G. Trawick, III Contracting Co." with a small team and a big vision. Over seven decades, we've grown into a respected woman-owned general contractor serving Alabama with distinction.

Today, under the leadership of Ginny Trawick, we continue our founder's commitment to building quality structures while keeping our jobsites safe and profitable. We specialize in competitively bid projects throughout Alabama with in-house HVAC capabilities.

Our Journey

Seven Decades of Building

1954
Founded as "Dan G. Trawick, III Contracting Co." with approximately five employees, focusing on fire-damaged building renovations and home construction.
1966
Obtained General Contractors License, expanding capabilities to include military contracts and larger commercial projects.
1973
Dan Trawick partnered with Clyde Pearce, forming "Pearce-Trawick Contractors" to specialize in military installation renovations and housing projects.
1983
Added HVAC service division "Pearce-Trawick Service," expanding our in-house capabilities to include full mechanical services.
1992
Officially incorporated as "Trawick Contractors Inc.," solidifying our identity and commitment to quality construction across Alabama.
2010
Ginny Trawick assumed management, continuing the family legacy and leading the company into a new era as a woman-owned contractor.
DBE Certified Contractor
Certified as a Disadvantaged Business Enterprise by the Birmingham Construction Industry Authority, demonstrating our commitment to integrity and excellence in the industry.
